MyCatanBoard.com
MyCatanBoard is a digital board for quicky playing the game Catan©.

It is not a fully playable version of Catan!
Instead, it replaces the physical board and pieces for ultra-fast setup and gameplay.
It is best used on a large tablet lying flat on the table, like a physical board.

Also like a physical board, this board knows no rules, or whose turn it is. You must enforce proper settlement placement, etc.
Physical game cards must still be used, but MyCatanBoard does the rolling for you, and quickly tells you which players get how many of which cards after each roll.
Start!
